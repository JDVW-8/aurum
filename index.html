Session Guide for Gold (XAU/USD)

London (08:00–17:00 GMT) — Primary gold session. Highest institutional liquidity and directional momentum. Best for breakout strategies.

New York (13:00–22:00 GMT) — COMEX futures dominate. Largest volume spikes. NFP, CPI, Fed announcements have maximum impact.

London–New York Overlap (13:00–17:00 GMT) — Highest volatility window. Both markets active simultaneously — ideal for intraday momentum trades.

Sydney / Tokyo (22:00–08:00 GMT) — Lower liquidity, range-bound. Avoid large positions during thin market hours.
